## Core Principles

### 1. Serve the Story
Every explicit scene must advance character development or plot. Avoid gratuitous content.

### 2. Power Dynamics
Explore consent, coercion, and control as central themes. The vampire-human dynamic is inherently about power.

### 3. Metaphorical Resonance
Use feeding as a metaphor for:
- Sexual intimacy
- Violence
- Addiction
- Power exchange

### 4. Character Agency
Characters must drive their own desires, even when destructive. Avoid passive victimization.

### 5. Consequences
Actions have emotional and physical repercussions. The dark world punishes and rewards in equal measure.

## YA Accessibility Maintenance

Despite explicit content, maintain YA core themes:
- **Identity exploration**: Who am I becoming?
- **First experiences**: Sexual, violent, independent
- **Social navigation**: Fitting in, standing out
- **Family separation**: Breaking from parents
- **Moral education**: Learning the world's cruelty

## Erotic Integration Guidelines

### Feeding Scenes
- Treat as intimate acts with sexual undertones
- Explore the vulnerability of the human
- The vampire's predatory nature as arousing and terrifying

### Blood Bonds
- Physical intimacy through blood exchange
- Family blood bonds as particularly taboo
- Addiction and dependence themes

### Transformation
- Body horror mixed with eroticism
- The change as a sexual awakening
- Loss of humanity as liberation and tragedy

## Content Warnings

All adapted content should be tagged with:
- **violence**: Physical harm, gore
- **sexual_content**: Explicit sexual acts
- **incest**: Family sexual dynamics
- **abuse**: Emotional, physical, psychological
- **substance**: Blood addiction, altered states
- **body_horror**: Transformation, violation of body
- **psychological**: Mental distress, trauma

## Writing Process

1. **Start with canon**: Understand the original scene/character
2. **Identify dark potential**: What can be made more complex, disturbing, or explicit?
3. **Maintain character voice**: Even in darkness, characters should sound like themselves
4. **Check YA alignment**: Does this still serve the coming-of-age narrative?
5. **Add content warnings**: Tag appropriately

## Example Transformation

**Original Scene**: Kiara has lunch with her family, feels embarrassed by their vampire nature.

**Dark Adaptation**: The lunch becomes a ritualized feeding where Kiara must participate in the family's blood consumption. The embarrassment transforms into arousal and shame. Henry's control over the ritual mirrors abusive family dynamics. Jacques's presence adds sexual tension.

**Taboo Elements**: Family blood sharing, Kiara's first adult feeding, Henry's patriarchal dominance
