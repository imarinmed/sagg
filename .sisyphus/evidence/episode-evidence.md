# Episode Evidence Log - Blood, Sweat, Tears (BST)

This log contains lore evidence extracted from the episode transcripts (s01e01–s01e07). 
*Note: s01e08 transcript provided was a duplicate of s01e03; evidence for e08 is limited to external context.*

## Episode 1: Kallblodig skolstart (Cold-blooded School Start)
- **Characters**:
    - **Kiara Natt och Dag**: Vampire (cold-blooded), Countess, rich, Swiss bank accounts, ballet dancer, 16 years old, never attended human school before. [s01e01]
    - **Alfred Carlsson**: Vampire researcher, influencer (~20 followers), has a fascination/fetish for vampires. [s01e01]
    - **Eric**: Team captain of Skaraborg Bats, chair of party committee, popular, dating Elise (intermittently). [s01e01]
    - **Elise**: Leader of Batgirls (good side), dating Eric. [s01e01]
    - **Chloe**: Member of Batgirls (evil side), believes she is the rightful leader. [s01e01]
    - **Jacques**: Kiara's brother, responsible for her behavior. [s01e01]
- **World Rules**:
    - **Blood System**: Symbiotic relationship. Humans donate blood to keep vampires calm; vampire blood is used as medicine for humans. [s01e01]
    - **Triggering**: Vampires get "triggered" and bloodthirsty by the combination of blood AND sweat. [s01e01]
    - **Terminology**: "Cold-blooded" is the politically correct term; "Vampire" (the V-word) is considered offensive by humans (though vampires are proud of it). [s01e01]
    - **Invitation Rule**: Vampires cannot enter a place unless invited. [s01e01]
    - **Truth Rule**: "A vampire always speaks the truth." [s01e01]
    - **Sun Protection**: Requires SPF 100+ and umbrellas. [s01e01]
    - **Crosses/Silver**: Vampires can handle crosses; real silver may be an issue. [s01e01]
- **Social Structures**:
    - **Skaraborg High**: Popularity is based on sports performance and contributions to the "Skaraborg Bats" trophy cabinet, not grades. [s01e01]
    - **Batgirls**: The school's dance crew, split into "good" and "evil" factions. [s01e01]
- **Locations**:
    - **Skaraborg High School**: Main setting. [s01e01]
- **Events**:
    - Kiara starts school; Principal orders an open audition for Batgirls to increase diversity. [s01e01]

## Episode 2: Audition till Batgirls (Audition for the Batgirls)
- **Characters**:
    - **Kiara's Mother**: Strict, traditional, dislikes the term "Mom" (prefers "Mother"). [s01e02]
    - **Didde**: Third-year student (repeated), has a car, popular but mocked. [s01e02]
    - **Kevin**: Friend of Didde. [s01e02]
- **World Rules**:
    - **Anti-trigger Medication**: Vampires take medication to control triggering. [s01e02]
    - **Greetings**: "Blood and honor" is a formal vampire greeting/parting. [s01e02]
- **Events**:
    - Kiara's mother warns her against sneaking out at night; Eric and Elise have relationship drama. [s01e02]

## Episode 3: Lunch hos Natt och Dag (Lunch at Natt och Dag's)
- **Characters**:
    - **Countess Dracula**: Kiara's grandmother; revealed to be the "brains" behind Count Dracula, whom she killed with a stake because he had three brides. [s01e03]
    - **Adam**: A male dancer in Batgirls, described as "poor" (cannot afford the 11,000 kr costume). [s01e03]
- **World Rules**:
    - **Family History**: The Natt och Dag family has lived in their estate since 1327. [s01e03]
    - **The Stake**: The stake that killed Count Dracula is kept as a family relic. [s01e03]
    - **Food**: Vampires serve "taccos" to humans to show appreciation for human culture. [s01e03]
    - **A-brunn**: Touching an A-brunn (manhole cover with 'A') is associated with bad luck or "anal sex" (local superstition). [s01e03]
- **Social Structures**:
    - **Batgirls**: Elite group; competition costumes cost 11,000 kr + VAT. [s01e03]
    - **Village Hall Party (Bygdegårdsfest)**: A local social event; Chloe tricks Kiara into overdressing for it. [s01e03]
- **Events**:
    - Alfred has lunch at the Natt och Dag estate; Kiara gets triggered by Eric's sweat in a sauna and attacks him. [s01e03]

## Episode 4: Matriarkatet (The Matriarchy)
- **Characters**:
    - **Jacques**: Kiara's brother; resentful of the inheritance rules. [s01e04]
- **World Rules**:
    - **Matriarchal Inheritance**: The youngest daughter inherits the family name, title, and all assets. Husbands must take the Natt och Dag name. [s01e04]
    - **Vampire Biology**: Vampires can see in the dark; they cry if they are truly in love. [s01e04]
    - **Peace Treaty**: A "blood pact" ended the era when humans and vampires killed each other. [s01e04]
    - **Vamp (Drug)**: Anti-trigger medicine used by humans as a high-potency drug ("orgasm for the brain"). [s01e04]
- **Locations**:
    - **Natt och Dag Cellar**: Contains an old sacrificial place and a secret escape route from the pre-peace era. [s01e04]
- **Events**:
    - Kiara hosts a party at the estate to fix her reputation; Kevin and Didde bring "Vamp" to the party. [s01e04]
