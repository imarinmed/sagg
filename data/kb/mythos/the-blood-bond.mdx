# The Blood Bond

## Summary

A mystical connection formed through blood exchange between vampires. The exact nature is not
fully explored in the series, but hints suggest deep emotional and possibly telepathic links
form between bonded parties.

Blood bonds appear to transcend ordinary relationships, creating connections that persist
across centuries and can only be severed by death—and perhaps not even then. The bond
carries obligations, creating a web of loyalty and dependency that structures vampire society.

## SST

### Description
In the dark adaptation, blood bonds become intensely possessive. The bonded can feel each
other's pleasure and pain, creating opportunities for both exquisite shared experiences
and cruel manipulations. Breaking a bond is not just difficult—it's psychologically devastating.

### Traits
- loss_of_emotional_autonomy

### Significance
Blood bonds blur the lines of individual identity. When you can feel your partner's
desires as your own, where does consent begin? The power dynamics in bonds between
vampires of different ages raise troubling questions about manipulation and control.

### Mechanics
Three exchanges create a partial bond. Seven exchanges create a full bond. Full bonds
allow emotional and sensory sharing. The older vampire in a bond typically has more
control over the connection, able to send sensations while blocking reception.

### Erotic Implications
Blood bonds transform intimacy into a shared experience. When bonded vampires feed from
each other during sex, both feel the pleasure of giving and receiving. This creates
unprecedented levels of connection—and vulnerability. Some vampires become addicted
to bonded feeding, unable to find satisfaction in ordinary encounters.
