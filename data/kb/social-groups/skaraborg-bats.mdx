# Skaraborg Bats

## Summary

The athletic heart of Skaraborg High. The Bats are more than just sports teams; they are the foundation of the school's social structure and the primary source of its human pride.

## BST

### Description
The Skaraborg Bats are the most prestigious group for male students at the school. Led by Eric, the team captain, the Bats are responsible for the school's impressive trophy collection. Their performance on the field directly translates to social power within the school. The Bats are often seen at the center of school parties and events, representing the "popular" human faction.

### Source Episodes
- s01e01: Eric introduced as team captain; trophy-based hierarchy explained.
- s01e05: Jacob mentioned as a sports star who won the scoring league.
- s01e07: Eric uses his status as a Bat to influence the school's policy on vampires.

## SST

### Description
The dark adaptation portrays the Skaraborg Bats as a group that values strength and aggression above all else. Their rivalry with the vampire population is rooted in a fear of being physically surpassed. The team's rituals and celebrations are depicted with a darker, more hedonistic tone, highlighting the "Level 2 - Emotional" and "Level 4 - Erotic" aspects of their competitive nature.
