## Overview

Educational integration represents a critical juncture in vampire-human coexistence. Schools serve as primary spaces where the two populations interact directly, establish relationships, and learn to navigate shared environments. The meaning and safety of this integration depends entirely on perspective and power structure.

## The Integration School Model

Public schools in the vampire-human integration framework intentionally mix both populations. Skaraborg High School exemplifies this model, with vampires attending alongside human students as a normalized part of school operations. For many young vampires, like Kiara in *Natt och Dag*, first attendance at human school marks a significant integration milestone.

### Curriculum & Language

The integrated curriculum is adapted to serve both populations:
- **Vampire history and culture** incorporated into standard education
- **PC terminology** taught explicitly as part of integration standards
- **Shared language** established for discussing vampire-human relations
- **Dual perspectives** integrated into humanities and social studies

### Mixed Athletics & Activities

Physical integration occurs through:
- **Mixed sports teams** (e.g., Skaraborg Bats)
- **Shared PE classes** and athletic competitions
- **Mixed social clubs** (e.g., Batgirls)
- **School events** designed as integration touchpoints (parties, sporting events, assemblies)

## The Question of Agency

The BST perspective emphasizes successful coexistence; the SST perspective interrogates whether this integration is genuinely consensual or systematically coercive. The distinction matters profoundly:

- Are humans choosing integration, or being conditioned toward acceptance?
- Are power differentials acknowledged and actively managed, or obscured by integration rhetoric?
- Does "mixed" schooling represent equality, or institutionalized exposure to physical dominance?
