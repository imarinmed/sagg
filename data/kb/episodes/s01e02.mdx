# Episode 2: Audition till Batgirls

## Summary

Kiara prepares for the Batgirls audition while navigating the complex social dynamics of Skaraborg High and the strict expectations of her vampire family.

## BST

### Key Events
- Kiara's friendship with Alfred deepens.
- Eric encourages Kiara to audition for the Batgirls.
- Kiara's mother warns her against sneaking out.
- Introduction of "Anti-trigger Medication" for vampires.

### Lore References
- **Blood and Honor**: A formal vampire greeting and parting.
- **Vamp Medication**: Used by vampires to control their bloodlust.

## SST

### Dark Adaptation
The episode explores "Level 2 - Emotional" darkness through the toxic relationship between Eric and Elise. Kiara is caught in the middle, her innocence being exploited by those around her. The "Anti-trigger Medication" is shown to have side effects that Kiara must hide, adding to her psychological burden.
