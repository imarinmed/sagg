# Episode 1: Kallblodig skolstart

## Summary

Kiara Natt och Dag begins her journey at Skaraborg High, facing social challenges and the pressure to hide her vampire nature while pursuing her passion for dance.

## BST

### Key Events
- Kiara starts school at Skaraborg High.
- Introduction of the "Blood System" and the symbiotic relationship between humans and vampires.
- The Principal mandates open auditions for the Batgirls.
- Kiara meets Alfred and Eric.

### Lore References
- **Cold-blooded**: The politically correct term for vampires.
- **Invitation Rule**: Vampires cannot enter a place unless invited.
- **Truth Rule**: Vampires always speak the truth.

## SST

### Dark Adaptation
The episode establishes the "Level 1 - Psychological" darkness of Kiara's integration. The school environment is more hostile, and the "Blood System" is shown to be a fragile peace maintained through mutual exploitation. Kiara's desire to dance is a metaphor for her desire to feel "alive" and human, despite her predatory nature.
