# Kiara Natt och Dag

## Summary

The 16-year-old vampire protagonist of *Blood, Sweat, Tears*. Born into the ancient Natt och Dag aristocracy, Kiara is a Countess and matriarchal heir navigating her first experience in human high school. A classically trained ballet dancer with dreams of human dancing and joining the Batgirls, she struggles to balance her vampire nature with her desire for integration and acceptance.

## BST

### Description

**Identity & Background**  
Kiara is introduced as a wealthy vampire Countess with Swiss bank accounts, raised in the Natt och Dag estate that has stood since 1327. [s01e01, s01e03] As the youngest daughter, she is the matriarchal heir under vampire tradition—inheriting the family name, title, and all assets. [s01e04] Despite her privileged upbringing, she has never attended human school before Skaraborg High. [s01e01]

**Physical Traits & Abilities**  
- **Dark Vision**: Can see in complete darkness [s01e04]
- **Emotional Crying**: Vampires only cry when truly in love [s01e04]
- **Sun Sensitivity**: Requires SPF 100+ sunscreen and umbrellas for outdoor activities [s01e01]
- **Triggering**: Becomes bloodthirsty when exposed to the combination of blood AND sweat [s01e01, s01e03]

**Aspirations & Struggles**  
Kiara dreams of human dancing and desperately wants to join the Batgirls, the school's elite dance crew split into "good" and "evil" factions. [s01e01, s01e02] Her ballet training makes her a natural dancer, but her vampire nature creates constant challenges. The Principal orders an open audition for Batgirls to increase diversity, giving Kiara her chance. [s01e01]

**Key Relationships**  
- **Alfred Carlsson**: The nerdy human vampire researcher becomes her closest friend and romantic interest. Their relationship progresses from curiosity to genuine connection. [s01e01-s01e07]
- **Jacques**: Her brother, who resents the matriarchal inheritance rules that favor her over him. He is tasked with monitoring her behavior. [s01e01, s01e04]
- **Eric**: Team captain of Skaraborg Bats. Kiara triggers and attacks him in a sauna after exposure to his sweat, leading to hospitalization. [s01e03, s01e06]
- **Elise**: Leader of the Batgirls (good side), becomes a mentor figure to Kiara. [s01e01, s01e07]
- **Chloe**: Batgirls rival who believes she should be the rightful leader and antagonizes Kiara. [s01e01, s01e02]

**Major Events**  
- **Episode 1**: Starts at Skaraborg High, applies for Batgirls audition
- **Episode 2**: Mother warns against sneaking out; Kiara continues pursuing Batgirls
- **Episode 3**: Hosts Alfred for lunch at estate, learns about grandmother's history (killed Dracula with a stake); triggers and attacks Eric in sauna
- **Episode 4**: Hosts party at estate to repair reputation after Eric incident; learns about matriarchal inheritance and blood pact history
- **Episode 5**: Dances with Eric at warehouse party, causing conflict with Elise
- **Episode 6**: Eric hospitalized from her bite; Principal bans vampires from masquerade
- **Episode 7**: Eric's speech about inclusion allows her to attend masquerade

## SST

### Description

In the dark adaptation, Kiara transforms from a naive vampire aristocrat into a figure caught between ancient power and modern desire. Her matriarchal inheritance becomes both a source of authority and a prison—expected to embody vampire tradition while craving the freedom of human experience.

**Erotic Dimensions**  
The triggering mechanism—blood and sweat combined—becomes a powerful metaphor for sexual arousal and loss of control. When Kiara attacks Eric in the sauna [s01e03], the adaptation frames it not just as danger but as an overwhelming erotic compulsion. Her ballet training, emphasizing control and discipline, creates a contrast with the raw, predatory hunger that emerges when triggered.

**Power Dynamics**  
Kiara's relationship with Alfred explores the complexities of vampire-human intimacy:
- **Consent & Compulsion**: The "vampire always speaks the truth" rule creates interesting dynamics around honesty and manipulation
- **Predator/Prey Reversal**: Alfred's fetish for vampires inverts traditional power roles
- **Observation & Performance**: Her ballet background translates to an awareness of being watched, performing desire

**Family Tensions**  
The matriarchal inheritance creates sexual undertones in family relationships:
- **Jacques's Resentment**: His frustration at being passed over creates tension charged with forbidden attraction (taboo blood bond implications)
- **Mother's Control**: Strict oversight of Kiara's behavior reflects anxiety about her sexuality awakening
- **Grandmother's Legacy**: The stake that killed Dracula symbolizes female power through penetration—a reversal of traditional vampire seduction

**Batgirls Rivalry**  
Competition with Chloe and Elise becomes not just about dance but about dominance, display, and desirability. The "good" vs "evil" faction split allows exploration of Kiara's dual nature—proper vampire aristocrat vs. uninhibited predator.
