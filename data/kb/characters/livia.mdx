# Livia

## Summary

A student at Skaraborg High and a dancer in the Batgirls crew. She is a secondary character who contributes to the school's social atmosphere and the competitive dance plot.

## BST

### Description
Livia is a dedicated member of the Batgirls. She is mentioned as managing choreography alongside Felicia. During the school's social events, she is part of the human crowd that reacts to the presence of vampires like Kiara.

### Source Episodes
- s01e05: Mentioned as managing choreography.
- s01e07: Seen at school events; Alfred is noted to be going with her (likely as a friend or date to the masquerade).

## SST

### Description
In the dark adaptation, Livia's character is used to show the human perspective of the "Blood Pact" era. She represents the innocence that is threatened by the proximity of "cold-blooded" predators, serving as a foil to characters like Alfred who are obsessed with them.
