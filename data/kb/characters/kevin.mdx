# Kevin

## Summary

A student at Skaraborg High and a close friend of Didde. Kevin is primarily known for his involvement in the distribution of "Vamp," a medication intended for vampires that has become a popular and dangerous drug among humans.

## BST

### Description
Kevin is a typical high school student who finds himself on the fringes of the school's social hierarchy, often following Didde's lead. He is instrumental in the "Vamp" drug plot, bringing the substance to Kiara's party and later selling stolen supplies at a warehouse party. His vulnerability is shown when he requires rescue by Elise.

### Source Episodes
- s01e02: Introduced as Didde's friend.
- s01e04: Brings "Vamp" to the Natt och Dag estate party.
- s01e05: Sells stolen "Vamp" at the warehouse party.
- s01e07: Saved by Elise from thugs.

## SST

### Description
In the dark adaptation, Kevin's character explores the themes of addiction and the exploitation of vampire biology. "Vamp" is treated with more gravity, and Kevin's role as a dealer places him in a position of both power and extreme risk. His relationship with the vampire world is one of parasitic opportunism, which eventually leads to his downfall or deeper corruption.
